Alice Dal Gobbo is assistant professor at the Department of Sociology and Social Research, University of Trento. Her work focuses on the study of everyday life, social movements and socio-technical innovations in the context of the multiple crises of the present. Apart from her theoretical interests in political ecology, in particular ecofeminist and decolonial approaches, her substantive work focuses on energy and food. She also pursues methodological reflections and innovation. She has published in international journals such as Environmental Values, Ethnography, Current Sociologyand her book "Everyday Life Ecologies: Sustainability, Crisis, Resistance" has been published by Lexington Books (Rowman and Littlefield) in 2023
