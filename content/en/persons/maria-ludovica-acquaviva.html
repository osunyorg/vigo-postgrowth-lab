Maria Ludovica Acquaviva enrolled in 2018 at the University of Naples Federico II in the Bachelor's program in Management Engineering for Logistics and Production. In March 2022 she obtained her Bachelor's degree, and for the following three months completed an internship at Laminazione Sottile S.p.A. in the Continuous Improvement department.

In September of the same year, she enrolled in the Master's program in Management Engineering, specializing in Green and Sustainable Manufacturing Management. In February 2024 Maria Ludovica started an Erasmus+ program in Poland, where she stayed for five months to complete her Master's exams. In September 2024 she moved to Pontevedra, Spain, to complete an internship and write her thesis at the University of Vigo, within the Post-Growth Innovation Lab group, thus concluding her academic journey.

Additionally, since 2019, she has been an alpine ski instructor, and she lately obtained the qualification of first-level coach.  For the past five years, she has practiced this profession every winter, which has been her passion since she was a child.
