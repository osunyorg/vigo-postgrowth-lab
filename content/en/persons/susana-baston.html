Susana Bastón is a Research Associate at the Post-Growth Innovation Lab - University of Vigo. She joined the group in the framework of the CoBlue project, granted by Reinforcing, a European central hub for promoting Open and Responsible R&I. The aim of this project is to work with stakeholders on common solutions to achieve the Mission Restore our Ocean and Waters objectives. Susana has a background in Marine Science and did a post-doc at Heriot-Watt University, at the Orkney Campus. Her research interest is the implementation of Marine Renewable Energy for a just transition, meeting environmental sustainability.
