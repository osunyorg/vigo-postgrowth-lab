Franca Marquardt is an anthropologist and documentarist studying emotions in internationalist movements from a feminist and political ecology perspective. Currently, she is a PhD candidate in Sociology and Political Science at the Scuola Normale Superiore in Florence. She holds a Bachelors in Visual Anthropology from Goldsmiths, University of London and a Masters in Anthropology from the University of Leipzig. She is also an editor of the ethnographic storytelling journal Thirdshelf.
