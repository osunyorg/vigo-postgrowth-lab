 Joana Amorim serves as an Invited Professor at ISCAP, Porto, Portugal, and holds the position of Associate of Operations at Granter.AI. Her educational background includes a bachelor's degree in Business Creativity and Innovation and a Master's degree in Entrepreneurship and Internationalization. Currently, Joana is pursuing her PhD at the University of Vigo, where her research focuses on the role of innovation competencies in driving the transition to a sustainable society.
During her academic journey, Joana lived in countries as Lithuania and Estonia, gaining valuable insights into diverse cultural perspectives. Additionally, she spent two months in Italy as part of the Explicit Project program, focusing on research concepts as circular economy and sustainability. These experiences have deepened her understanding of global dynamics and reinforced her commitment to sustainability research.
