Joana Amorim is an Invited Professor at ISCAP and an R&D Consultant. She is currently pursuing her doctoral degree at the University of Vigo while also working as a researcher at CEOS. PP. With a forward-thinking approach and extensive global experience, she injects vibrant energy and a new perspective into business consulting. Her interests lie in business dynamics and interpersonal interactions. She holds a Bachelor's degree in Creativity and Business Innovations, a Master's degree in Entrepreneurship and Internationalization, and is presently a Ph. D. candidate in Economic Analysis and Business Strategy. Having resided in countries such as Lithuania, Estonia, and Italy, she has conducted research across diverse fields, including marketing, financial systems, emotional intelligence, leadership, and circular economy.
