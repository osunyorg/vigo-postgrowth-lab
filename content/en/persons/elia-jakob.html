Elia is a student of the Hochschule für Gesellschaftsgestaltung (HfGG) in Koblenz in Germany since 2022. He is pursuing his bachelor’s degree in „ economics - sustainability - transformation“. Main topics of his studies are Degrowth, feminist economics and plural economics. Besides his studies is Elia member of the board of the „Verein für Gesellschaftsgestaltung“. There he is part of the finance and organizing team.

Between the beginning of November and the end of March will Elia do an internship in the the Post Growth Innovation Lab. He will help in the research of Mario Pansera to technological Innovation in a post-growth era. There he will revise the existing database and bring in the German speaking research to the topic.
