He holds a BSc and MSc in Philosophy from the University of Parma, IT, and studied at the University of Complutense, Buenos Aires and Paris VIII - Nanterre, for one year each. He received the Laura Conti Political Ecology Prize for his Master thesis on Eco-Marxist debates, and published it as a book in Italian titled "Marxismo ed ecologia. Origine e sviluppo di un dibattito globale".Jacopo was involved in ecological, student and anti-eviction movements. He is interested in the critique of political economy, social and ecological justice, theory of hegemony. Over the next few years with PROSPERA, he will research circular economy with a comparison between UE and Chinese models.
