  Carlos is an Associated Professor at University of Vigo. His work focuses on Cooperatives, firms that promotes social values as democracy and equality.  His latest research focus on to what extent stakeholders' behaviour meets such values addressing economic and financial objectives at the same time. She is currently involved in the project SAVES that aims to make economic and financial  information of coops in Galicia (Spain) accessible to the public and to promote research on Cooperatives and Social Economy. The first results are already available at https://saves.uvigo.gal.
