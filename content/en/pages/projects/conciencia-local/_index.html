ConCiencia Local aims to integrate Science for Policy (S4P) into local authorities, thereby helping to improve public policy.

body: >-


The project proposes the creation of a working group in the form of a Municipal Scientific Advisory Committee (CACiM) within Pontevedra City Council, composed of research staff from the University of Vigo and municipal administration staff (members of the local government and technical staff).
The main objective is to promote the establishment of spaces for knowledge exchange and the use of scientific evidence in public decision-making and the implementation of municipal policies, through a pilot project focused on a specific initiative or issue of interest to the local administration. Using innovative participatory methods (such as ‘serious games’ or art-based activities), the CACiM’s mission will be to co-create an action plan with recommendations grounded in scientific knowledge, which the council can use to address the selected initiative or issue.
The project will also enable the development of an implementation guide detailing the methodology used, so that it can be replicated to address other needs within Pontevedra City Council, or so that any other municipality in Galicia or Spain can launch a similar project.
The initiative will be evaluated throughout the project, and all information and data generated will be recorded for a research study on this type of public innovation strategy, with the findings to be published in a scientific journal.
