Víctor José Sánchez will take part in the panel discussion at the event entitled 'Pint of Science' in Pontevedra, which is being held simultaneously in bars across more than 100 towns and cities in Spain. The talk, entitled T·I·A·S This Is Also Science, will focus on degrowth and related concepts such as climate justice, the right to repair, responsible innovation, and other issues we are working on in the research group. His talk will take place on Tuesday 19th at around 8 pm, although the event starts at 7 pm, in the Nasa tavern.
