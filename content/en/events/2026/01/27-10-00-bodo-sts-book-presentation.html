Mario Pansera will be in Nord University (Bodø, Norway) givin his research seminar intended for PhD students and academic staff working on topics related to  organization studies, sustainability, innovation studies, science and technology studies (STS), and responsible research and innovation (RRI) . Participants are invited to submit  work-in-progress papers  for feedback and discussion. Priority will be given to papers aligned with these themes or with potential fit for the journal  Organization & Environment  (ABS level 3).Prof.  Mario Pansera  will offer insights from his work as  Deputy Editor of Organization & Environment , and provide constructive feedback on submitted manuscripts. The seminar aims to support early-career researchers in the publication process and foster collaboration across research groups.The seminar will begin with a brief overview of Mario's work and the Post-growth Innovation Lab, followed by an introduction to the Organization & Environment journal.
