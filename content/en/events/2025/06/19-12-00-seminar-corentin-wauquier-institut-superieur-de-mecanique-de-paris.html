  Abstract : After almost 3 and a half months in Pontevedra I think it's time for a review. I wanted to share what I have been working on, what I learned and what I expect for the future of my internship which will continue back in France. I have still a lot to learn and I wanted to take the opportunity of this summary to engage and ask everyone that will want to come their ideas and opinions on what I should focus on for the next part.
