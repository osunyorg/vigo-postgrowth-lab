We would like to invite you to the SERIGO Community of Practice (CoP) launch webinar, taking place online on 21st November from 13:00 to 14:00 CET. This event marks the beginning of the CoP activities, dedicated to social innovation for inclusion of vulnerable groups in rural areas.

body: >-


The SERIGO CoP is a dynamic platform that connects professionals,
researchers, policymakers, and practitioners from diverse backgrounds. By
joining, you will have the opportunity to collaborate with peers, share
insights, and contribute to shaping social innovations that have a lasting
impact on rural development.During the webinar, you will:
Discover
SERIGO’s objectives, key activities, and expected outcomes,
Learn about
the role of our Community of Practice (CoP) and how it supports
knowledge-sharing and collaboration,

Understand
how you can contribute to and benefit from being part of this vibrant community.We invite you to register here for the launch event, and we would be thrilled to welcome you as a
member of the SERIGO CoP.

If
you are interested in innovative solutions to strengthen rural communities,
this is the perfect opportunity to get involved!
SERIGO is an EU-supported project focused on fostering social innovations
driven by the social economy. Our mission is to strengthen the
resilience of rural communities across Europe, promote social inclusion, and
enhance well-being through innovative, community-driven approaches.More about SERIGO: https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/projects/serigo/

The SERIGO CoP is a dynamic platform that connects professionals, researchers, policymakers, and practitioners from diverse backgrounds. By joining, you will have the opportunity to collaborate with peers, share insights, and contribute to shaping social innovations that have a lasting impact on rural development.

During the webinar, you will:

  • Discover SERIGO’s objectives, key activities, and expected outcomes,
  • Learn about the role of our Community of Practice (CoP) and how it supports knowledge-sharing and collaboration,

Understand how you can contribute to and benefit from being part of this vibrant community.

We invite you to register here for the launch event, and we would be thrilled to welcome you as a member of the SERIGO CoP.

If you are interested in innovative solutions to strengthen rural communities, this is the perfect opportunity to get involved!

notes: >-

SERIGO is an EU-supported project focused on fostering social innovations driven by the social economy. Our mission is to strengthen the resilience of rural communities across Europe, promote social inclusion, and enhance well-being through innovative, community-driven approaches.

More about SERIGO: https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/projects/serigo/
