The 9th International Degrowth Conference will be held in Zagreb (Croatia) between August 29 and September 2,2023, under the theme “Planet, People, Care: It Spells Degrowth!”

Our researchers David Soto, Brais Suárez, and Elisa Schramm will participate in the thematic sessions “Technology and science for degrowth” and “Degrowth as a political project“, presenting their latest papers: (Re-)Imagining Technology and Innovation for Degrowth and The role of the circular economy in the 2020s’ critical juncture: Path-fixer, path-opener or path-breaker?
