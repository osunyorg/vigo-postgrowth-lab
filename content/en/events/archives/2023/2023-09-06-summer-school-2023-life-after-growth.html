6-9 Set 2023 Kalentzi (Greece)

The Post-growth Innovation Lab together with the P2P Lab of Tallinn University of Technology, and the Department of Social Policy of the University of Thrace is going to co-host a summer school this September in Greece.  Titled “Life after Growth”, the objetive is to seek to re-establish how we value, care for, and nurture our collective affairs through the commons.

Participation is free of charge.
