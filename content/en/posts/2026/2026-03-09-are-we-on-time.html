On 6 March, the SEPA-interea group at the University of Santiago de Compostela organised the colloquium ‘Are we on time? What are we on time for?’, inviting reflection on education and the socio-environmental crisis.
The session included Edgar González Gaudiano from the University of Veracruz (Mexico) and Júlio J. Conde from the Post-Growth Innovation Lab, and was moderated by Pablo Meira Cartea.
The meeting aimed to create a space for dialogue on the role of environmental education in a context of ecological and social emergency, emphasising the potential of education to generate new questions, critical perspectives, and possibilities for collective action.
