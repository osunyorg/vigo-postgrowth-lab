  Our colleague Júlio Conde participated last Friday afternoon in a colloquium on education and the socio-environmental crisis, held at the University of Santiago de Compostela (USC) and organised by the Social Pedagogy and Environmental Education Research Group.

  The event was moderated by Pablo Ángel Meira Cartea (SEPA-interea Research Group) and featured the following panellists: Edgar Javier González Gaudiano (Veracruzana University, Mexico) and our researcher Júlio J. Conde Lopes (Post-Growth Innovation Lab, University of Vigo).
