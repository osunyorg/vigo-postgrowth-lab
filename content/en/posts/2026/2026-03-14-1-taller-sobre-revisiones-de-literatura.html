  Online training session delivered to the ATTRACT research team, focused on the methodological design, development, and writing of systematic literature reviews linked to the project's scientific objectives. The session included practical training on formulating review questions, bibliographic search strategies, inclusion and exclusion criteria, corpus construction and refinement, selection protocols, coding and thematic analysis of the literature, as well as the writing of findings and the development of research agendas.
