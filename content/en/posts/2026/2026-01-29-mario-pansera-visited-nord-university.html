body: >-


Mario Pansera, researcher and director of the Post-Growth Innovation Lab, visited Nord University in Norway from 26 to 28 January to take part in various activities. During his stay, Pansera presented the group's work and learned about projects being undertaken by Nord University and Nordlab (Centre for Crisis Management and Collaboration). He also delivered a research seminar, sharing his perspectives on publishing in journals the field of organization studies with pre-doctoral and junior researchers, drawing on his experience as a researcher and editor. In addition, he presented the book he recently co-authored with Javier Lloveras, Post Growth Innovation: Resisting the Pursuit of Endless Economic Growth . The visit also included participation in a workshop where, together with colleagues from other centres and universities, he worked on preparing a proposal to create a Doctoral Network within the framework of the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Actions call for proposals.
