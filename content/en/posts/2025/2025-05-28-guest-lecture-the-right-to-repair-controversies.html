Guest lecture delivered as part of the University Master's Degree in Circular Economy at the Universidade de Vigo, taught by Javier Lloveras. Based on ATTRACT, the session introduced students to current debates surrounding the right to repair, with particular attention to its social, regulatory, business, and environmental dimensions. Drawing on the objectives of the ATTRACT project, the lecture explored controversies related to repair practices, product durability, corporate strategies, citizen demands, and the broader challenges involved in transitioning towards more sustainable models of consumption.
