body: >-


As part of the methodological
preparation for the ATTRACT project, Javier Lloveras led a seminar for
participants, focusing on the use of interviews in qualitative research. The
session covered the main types of interviews, their specific characteristics,
and the contexts in which each approach is most appropriate.

 

The seminar also addressed
the essential steps of the interview process, including planning, data
collection, and key methodological and ethical considerations. This training is
especially relevant now that ATTRACT is moving into the interview phase, as it
ensures a common methodological framework and strengthens the rigor and
coherence of the project's qualitative research on the Right to Repair (R2R).

As part of the methodological preparation for the ATTRACT project, Javier Lloveras led a seminar for participants, focusing on the use of interviews in qualitative research. The session covered the main types of interviews, their specific characteristics, and the contexts in which each approach is most appropriate.   The seminar also addressed the essential steps of the interview process, including planning, data collection, and key methodological and ethical considerations. This training is especially relevant now that ATTRACT is moving into the interview phase, as it ensures a common methodological framework and strengthens the rigor and coherence of the project's qualitative research on the Right to Repair (R2R).
