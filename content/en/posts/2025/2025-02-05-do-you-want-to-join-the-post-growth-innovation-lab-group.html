The following job opportunities are currently open:

- Xunta de Galicia Postdoctoral Fellowships: the Galician government has published a call for post-doctoral researchers who wish to join a research group at Galician universities and research centres.

- Research assistant for the CoBlue project: Brais Suárez is looking for a support technician for the CoBlue project (Co-creating knowledge and politics in the blue economy), funded by the European Reinforcing project.

- Research assistant (graduate) for the ATTRACT project: Javier Lloveras and Mario Pansera are looking for a research assistant for the project ATTRACT - A Right to a Repair Society: Controversies, Opportunities and Challenges, funded by the Ministry of Science, Innovation and Universities

All information can be found on our website https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/team/job-offers/ (external link)
