In April, the Post-growth Innovation Lab hosted a workshop on ‘Post-growth/degrowth organisation” within the framework of our project PROSPERA. The workshop brought together over 20 international researchers exploring what post-growth and degrowth means for organisations and organising during 3 intensive days.
