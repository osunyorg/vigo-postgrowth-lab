This week we will be attending the ESEE-Degrowth International Conference 2023 in Zagreb (Croatia). The event titled “Planet, People, Care: It Spells Degrowth!” will bring together activists, artists, academics, practitioners, political representatives, and the general public. For further information visit the Conferente website

Our researchers David Soto, Brais Suárez, and Elisa Schramm will participate in the thematic sessions “Technology and science for degrowth” and “Degrowth as a political project“, presenting their latest papers: (Re-)Imagining Technology and Innovation for Degrowth and The role of the circular economy in the 2020s’ critical juncture: Path-fixer, path-opener or path-breaker?

Next edition 2024, 10th International Degrowth Conference, will be organized by University of Vigo in the city of Pontevedra. It will take place jointly with the 15th Conference of the European Society for Ecological Economics Check out the International ESEE – Degrowth Conference 2024 Pontevedra website to keep updated.
