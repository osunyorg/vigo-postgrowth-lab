For this team, 2024 was a memorable year in which great things happened: ☑️ We hosted the ESEE-Degrowth 2024 International Conference . With more than 1200 participants, we are very proud of the work done and the unforgettable moments we experienced last June ( https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/news/2024-10-08-esee-degrowth-2024-video/ ). ✅ We have successfully completed two of our projects :
The JUST2CE project, funded by the EU ( https://just2ce.eu/ ) 
The Post-Growth S.A. - Pontevedra project, co-funded by the Fundación Española para la Ciencia y la Tecnología, FECYT ( https://postgrowthsa.webs.uvigo.es/postgrowthsapontevedra/ )
☑️ Two new projects have been launched:
The SERIGO Project , funded by the EU ( https://www.serigo-project.eu/ )
The Post-Growth S.A. - Vigo project, co-funded by the FECYT ( https://postgrowthsa.webs.uvigo.es/postgrowthsavigo/ )
✅ We have received funding to start two new projects next year :
The ATTRACT project, funded by the Ministerio de Ciencia, Innovación y Universidades
The CoBlue project, funded as part of the cascade call for proposals of the European REINFORCING Project
☑️ 25 new publications have been issued, including articles, books and book chapters ( https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/publications/ ) ✅ We have further strengthened the internationalisation of the group by receiving the visit of 9 researchers from centres abroad and carrying out research stays in 8 centres from 5 different countries. ☑️ We have added new members to our team: Aitor Alonso Rodríguez, Tess Doezema, Ana García Quintana, Sebastián Iturriaga Gazol and Carmen Rey Mayo. All these successes are thanks to the great team that made them possible. We're going on holiday for a few days to recharge our batteries, but we're looking forward to coming back and seeing what 2️⃣0️⃣2️⃣5️⃣ has in store for us. 🫎🌲 We wish you a Merry Christmas and a Happy, Fair and Degrowth New Year !

For this team, 2024 was a memorable year in which great things happened:

☑️ We hosted the ESEE-Degrowth 2024 International Conference (external link). With more than 1200 participants, we are very proud of the work done and the unforgettable moments we experienced last June (https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/news/2024-10-08-esee-degrowth-2024-video/ (external link)).

✅ We have successfully completed two of our projects:

  • The JUST2CE project, funded by the EU (https://just2ce.eu/ (external link)) 
  • The Post-Growth S.A. - Pontevedra project, co-funded by the Fundación Española para la Ciencia y la Tecnología, FECYT (https://postgrowthsa.webs.uvigo.es/postgrowthsapontevedra/ (external link))

☑️ Two new projects have been launched:

  • The SERIGO Project, funded by the EU (https://www.serigo-project.eu/ (external link))
  • The Post-Growth S.A. - Vigo project, co-funded by the FECYT (https://postgrowthsa.webs.uvigo.es/postgrowthsavigo/ (external link))

✅ We have received funding to start two new projects next year:

  • The ATTRACT project, funded by the Ministerio de Ciencia, Innovación y Universidades
  • The CoBlue project, funded as part of the cascade call for proposals of the European REINFORCING Project

☑️ 25 new publications have been issued, including articles, books and book chapters (https://postgrowth-lab.uvigo.es/publications/ (external link))

✅ We have further strengthened the internationalisation of the group by receiving the visit of 9 researchers from centres abroad and carrying out research stays in 8 centres from 5 different countries.

☑️ We have added new members to our team: Aitor Alonso Rodríguez, Tess Doezema, Ana García Quintana, Sebastián Iturriaga Gazol and Carmen Rey Mayo.

All these successes are thanks to the great team (external link) that made them possible.

We're going on holiday for a few days to recharge our batteries, but we're looking forward to coming back and seeing what 2️⃣0️⃣2️⃣5️⃣ has in store for us.

🫎🌲We wish you a Merry Christmas and a Happy, Fair and Degrowth New Year!
